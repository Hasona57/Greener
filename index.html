About Greener

Greener is an app designed to help you reduce waste and promote sustainable living. Whether you're at home or on the go, Greener provides you with tips, tools, and challenges to minimize your environmental footprint.

Features

Waste Tracker

Monitor your daily waste output and track your progress in reducing it over time.

Recycling Guide

Find detailed information on how to properly recycle different materials.

Eco-friendly Product Finder

Discover sustainable products that are good for the planet and your home.

Daily Challenges

Participate in daily eco-challenges to stay motivated and engaged in your green journey.

Carbon Footprint Calculator

Calculate your carbon footprint and get tips on how to reduce it effectively.

Community Events

Join local events and initiatives aimed at making your community greener.
